SALPay Mobile Redesign


One stop for all your finance needs
SALPay provides you tools to manage your finances, pay your bills, shop, save, and apply for loans under one application. SALPay is connected with Salarium, allowing for the transition from payroll to payout more seamless and hassle-free.




Redesign objectives
Redesigning of SALPay Mobile was focused on transforming the outdated look of the existing application and give it more personality and branding closer to the goal of what Salarium wants SALPay to be; which is to have a better tone and branding to appeal to a wider spectrum of audiences.

Scalability was also a main objective of the redesigning that was targeted. As SALPay expands its features further, we wanted to make sure that as more features, more billers, and more partners came along the line, the design was easily adjustable to meet these needs.
