SALPay Web Design


One stop for all your finance needs
SALPay provides you tools to manage your finances, pay your bills, shop, save, and apply for loans under one application. SALPay is connected with Salarium, allowing for the transition from payroll to payout more seamless and hassle-free.




Design objectives
Designing of the SALPay Web was focused around having another platform users could use SALPay on, since its launch, SALPay only had the SALPay mobile app to use. Branching out to web provides more accessibility to users.

The main design objective was to translate the SALPay Mobile Rebrand to a web version. They share their major objectives in terms of applying UI/UX trends, have a better tone and branding to appeal to a wider spectrum of audiences.
